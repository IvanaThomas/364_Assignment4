Edgar's macaroni and cheese recipe:
Yield: ½ hotel pan, 12 – 6oz. servings
1 lb. macaroni
TT salt
1-quart Cheese Sauce
TT Worcestershire sauce
TT Tabasco sauce
½ lb. Cheddar cheese, grated
½ lb. Gruyere, grated
2 oz. whole butter, plus butter for baking pan
1 lb. shiitake mushrooms, cleaned, trimmed and sliced
Candied bacon – see separate recipe
4 oz. bread crumbs
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Cook the macaroni in salted water until done to al dente. Drain, refresh in cold water, drain and
reserve.
3. Season the Cheese Sauce with the Worcestershire and Tabasco sauces. Mix the macaroni with
the Cheese Sauce and Cheddar.
4. Cook mushrooms: In a sauté pan, melt 1oz. butter. Add mushrooms and sauté until cooked
through. Drain. Add to macaroni mixture.
5. Butter individual baking cups, gratins, or a half hotel pan using whole butter. Pour the macaroni
mixture into the container(s).
6. Melt the remaining butter in a small sauté pan. Add the bread crumbs and toss to coat. Sprinkle
the bread crumbs evenly over the macaroni.
7. Bake uncovered until hot, approximately 30 minutes.
Candied Bacon
1. 2. 3. 4. 5. 1 lb. bacon
2 oz. brown sugar
TT cayenne pepper
Preheat the oven to 400°.
Line 1-2 rimmed baking sheets with parchment. Insert bacon rack.
In a small bowl, whisk the brown sugar with the chile powder.
Arrange the bacon strips on the rack and coat the tops with the chile sugar.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until caramelized and almost crisp.
Cheese Sauce
Yield: 1 qt.
1 qt. Bechamel
2 oz. grated Monterey Jack
2 oz. grated Parmesan
Dash Worcestershire sauce
¾ tsp. dry mustard
Add grated cheese and seasonings to hot béchamel, stirring until cheese is melted.

Yield: 1 qt.
1 qt. milk
2 oz. flour
2 oz. butter
TT salt and pepper
TT grated nutmeg
1. Heat milk.
2. 3. In a separate sauce pan, make a white roux with the flour and butter.
